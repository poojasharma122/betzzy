How to Bet Online in Ireland

Betting online in Ireland is easy, but knowing the basics will help you make smarter choices and enjoy a safer experience. This guide walks you through the key steps — from picking a bookmaker to managing your bankroll responsibly.

Choose a Licensed Irish Betting Site

Always use a bookmaker licensed by the Irish Revenue Commissioners. This ensures your bets are protected under Irish law.

Some trusted names include:

  • Paddy Power

  • BoyleSports

  • Bet365

  • Ladbrokes

  • 888sport

Create an Account

You’ll need to register by providing:

  • Name and date of birth (you must be 18+)
  • Proof of address
  • Email and mobile number

Most bookmakers verify your identity before allowing withdrawals.

Deposit Funds

Popular payment options in Ireland include:

  • Visa or Mastercard
  • PayPal, Skrill, Neteller
  • Bank transfer
  • Cash in betting shops (with BoyleSports or Ladbrokes)

Most deposits are instant, but bank transfers may take longer. Always check the minimum deposit amount and any fees with your bookmaker.

Understand Betting Markets

Common bet types include:

  • Match Result (1X2) – Win, draw, or lose
  • Over/Under Goals – Predict total goals in a match
  • Handicap Betting – Giving one team a virtual head start
  • Accumulators – Combine multiple selections for higher odds
  • In-Play Betting – Place bets during a live event

Take Advantage of Promotions

Most Irish bookmakers offer:

  • Free bet sign-up bonuses
  • Odds boosts on selected matches
  • Money-back specials on certain outcomes

Always read the terms and conditions to understand wagering requirements.

Manage Your Bankroll

Set a weekly or monthly budget

Avoid chasing losses

Bet smaller amounts more often to reduce risk

Bet Responsibly

Betting should be fun, not a source of stress. If you ever feel your gambling is getting out of control, seek help from Gambling Care on 1800 936 725 or visit www.gamblingcare.ie.
