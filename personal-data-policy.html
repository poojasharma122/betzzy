Personal Data Policy

Betzzy (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This Personal Data Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information in line with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (“GDPR”) and the Data Protection Acts 1988–2018 of Ireland. This policy is supplementary to our Privacy Policy and provides further detail on our handling of personal data.

1. Data Controller

Betzzy is the data controller for all personal data collected via betzzy.com.

Contact:
Email: info@betzzy.com

2. Categories of Personal Data We Process

  • Identity Data – Name, username, or similar identifiers (only when voluntarily provided).
  • Contact Data – Email address or other contact information you choose to share with us.
  • Technical Data – IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, pages visited, and referral source.
  • Usage Data – Information on how you interact with our website, such as clicks, navigation paths, and session duration.
  • Marketing Preferences – Consent records for email newsletters or promotional communications.

3. How We Collect Personal Data

  • Direct interactions (e.g., when you email us, sign up for updates, or submit an enquiry form).
  • Automated technologies (e.g., cookies, analytics tools).
  • Affiliate link tracking systems (for measuring referral performance, without collecting betting account details).

4. Purposes of Processing

  • Providing and maintaining our website content and comparison services.
  • Responding to user enquiries.
  • Sending marketing or promotional communications (only with consent).
  • Analysing website performance and optimising content.
  • Meeting legal or regulatory obligations.

5. Lawful Basis for Processing

  • Consent – For marketing communications and non-essential cookies.
  • Legitimate Interests – For site performance monitoring, fraud prevention, and content improvement.
  • Legal Obligation – To comply with laws or respond to lawful requests.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law.

  • Technical and usage data is typically retained for up to 26 months.
  • Marketing preference records are retained until you withdraw consent.

7. Data Sharing

  • Trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, email platforms) bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Regulatory or legal authorities when required by law.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

8. International Data Transfers

Where data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we implement GDPR-compliant safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or transfers to jurisdictions with adequacy decisions.

9. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. These include encryption, access controls, and secure hosting infrastructure.

10. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request erasure (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Request data portability.

Requests can be submitted via info@betzzy.com. We will respond within one month, as required by GDPR.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Personal Data Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on betzzy.com with the “Effective Date” clearly stated.
