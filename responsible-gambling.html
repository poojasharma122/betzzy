Responsible Gambling

Responsible Gambling at Betzzy

At Betzzy, we believe betting should always be enjoyable, never harmful. Our role as a comparison site is to promote safe gambling habits and direct players to support if they need it. We only feature licensed Irish betting operators that follow strict player protection measures.

Our Commitment to Safe Gambling

We encourage all visitors to:

  • Set limits on deposits, losses, and playing time
  • Take regular breaks from betting activities
  • Only gamble with money you can afford to lose
  • Avoid betting under the influence of alcohol or when stressed

Recognising Problem Gambling

Signs that gambling may be becoming a problem include:

  • Spending more time or money on betting than planned
  • Chasing losses with bigger bets
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund betting
  • Neglecting work, relationships, or other responsibilities

If any of these signs sound familiar, it may be time to take a break and seek help.

Support & Help Services in Ireland

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling, confidential support is available:

  • Gambling Care – Freephone: 1800 936 725 | Website: www.gamblingcare.ie
  • Problem Gambling Ireland – Website: www.problemgambling.ie
  • Gamblers Anonymous Ireland – Website: www.gamblersanonymous.ie

Tools Available from Licensed Irish Bookmakers

Most licensed betting sites in Ireland offer responsible gambling tools such as:

  • Deposit limits
  • Loss limits
  • Reality checks
  • Self-exclusion options
  • Account closure on request

We encourage you to use these tools to stay in control of your betting.

Final Word

Gambling is a form of entertainment, not a way to make money. Play for fun, know your limits, and never risk your financial stability. If betting stops being enjoyable, stop and seek support.
